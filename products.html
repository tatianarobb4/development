If This World were Mine

By E. Lynn Harris

This book kicked off my search of 'black people in the 90s just doing things' books. Not just a group of women but men and women. Which was super refreshing. Even though I didn't like the tech guy. He was a HOTEP.. I had my two obvious favorites: successful and sexy black woman and successful and sexy gay black man. And they were best friends! Also can I just say that the cover is stunning!

Waiting to Exhale

By Terry McMillian

A great book. All of the women are dynamic, different, and exciting in their own ways. I felt like I was friends with them. I think I read this book in two days. It was impossible for me to put down. Every character is relatable in different ways. We had my pretty astrology babe, rich yet kinda insecure divorcee, single mom trying to find herself again, and career woman also looking for love.
